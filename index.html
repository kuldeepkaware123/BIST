All that you need to know about SIH - Inter BIST

For the selection of SIH, this year an internal hackathon is being organized by Coding Club of BIST. Any team from BIST who wishes to be part of SIH 2023, needs to qualify the internal hackathon

Some important rules to keep in mind are:

  1. The team is to be of compulsory 6 members with 1 girl compulsory in the team.
  2. Select the problem statement from the SIH website: https://d2c.pw/X7XxV
  3. One team can have only one problem statement.

  5. If a person is found in two teams, both teams may get disqualified.
  6. Remember: Only 5 teams for software and 2 teams for hardware will be able to register for SIH 2023.
  7. The final result would be declared by 15 Feb. Post that the OSS club will also help in enhancing the presentation.
  8. For details regarding Idea Presentation: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=U8tElWl5ISE
